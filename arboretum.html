Brad Balliet's 'Arboretum' is a piece for multiple bassoon soloists designed to be played in a forest of trees of multiple species. Each bassoonist is assigned a specific tree, and plays a part associated with that type of tree. As the listener walks through the arboretum, they hear a unique blend of bassoon voices as parts fade in and out, sometimes rising above the forest.

Coming soon: A web applet that will simulate the unique experience for those who could not attend the live performance!
